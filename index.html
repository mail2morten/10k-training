Injury Prevention & Pro Tips

Monday "Shakeouts"

After Sunday's long run, legs feel heavy. Keep this 2k/3k incredibly slow. Its only job is to increase blood flow and flush lactic acid before Tuesday's full rest.

Friday is "Quality"

Because Saturday is a rest day, afford to push yourself hard during Friday's intervals & tempo runs. Don't worry about being too tired for Sunday's Long Run.

Hydration & Fuel

Hardest efforts fall on Wed, Fri, and Sun. Ensure you eat enough complex carbs and drink plenty of water the day before each of these critical sessions.

Alcohol-Free Recovery

Cutting alcohol significantly improves sleep architecture, hydration levels, and muscle protein synthesis. Use the toggles to track your successful weeks!
